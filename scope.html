Web Serial needs a Chromium-based browser (Chrome / Edge / Brave / Chromium) and a secure context (HTTPS or localhost). Click Connect and pick the ESP32 port. The spectrogram scrolls right-to-left — newest audio on the right — with frequency on the vertical axis and energy mapped to color (the legend is dBFS). The readout top-right is an estimated acoustic dB SPL. Floor sets the quietest level the colormap shows: raise it to cut background haze, lower it to reveal faint detail.
